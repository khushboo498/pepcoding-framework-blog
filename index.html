Frameworks

What are frameworks?

Frameworks are tools that are there to make a coder’s life easier and make their work efficient. How? Imagine you are writing a story. But every time you make a typo you must start all over again. Or you are cycling down the street but at every bump the cycle breaks to pieces and you must build it all over again. Does that sound tempting?

Exactly. Frameworks are things that are designed to make programmers’ life less troublesome by providing solutions to already dealt with problems in coding. But it is important to remember that unlike libraries that give solutions to a specific problem, they form the base of your application.

What do they do?

Solving common problems

Routing and templating, two of the most common problems that web applications need to solve, are swiftly handled by the frameworks.

They basically prevent you from reinventing the wheel, which in the software world means solving the common problems that already have a ready-made solution.

Enhance the quality

If you have already learned the languages, learning frameworks would be easier as these frameworks are also made by coding languages. When applied, they fulfil their specific purpose. Since frameworks take care of the primary things, you can direct your creativity towards creating better user interface design, better performing functions and smoother user experience by using threads.

Strengthen security

Using framework solutions are much more safe and secure. That’s why frameworks are quite often used for a variety of purposes such as authentication, authorization, email verification, etc.

Types of Frameworks

There are a variety of frameworks for the diverse needs that occur in the programming world. These are divided in the following 3 categories.

Web frameworks

These are the most utilized frameworks. Back end web frameworks help people build web applications. Commonly used web frameworks are Ruby on rails, Laravel, Django and Angular.

Front end framework

Front-end frameworks are used by developers using technologies like html, CSS and Javascript. To create nice applications. And everything is well executed in the user's browser.

Most popular ones are angular and react.

Mobile app development frameworks

Now it is one of the most in demand skills because of the growing web development market. Flutter, Xamarin, Ionic, React and Native are the most used frameworks in this category.
